About Us


Building Excellence, Designing the Future

Founded in 2014, Madani Development Engineering Consultancy is a full-service engineering and architectural firm based in Barka, Oman. We specialize in architectural planning, structural design, 3D modeling, interior design, and site supervision, delivering innovative and sustainable solutions tailored to our clients' needs.

With a focus on quality, functionality, and aesthetic appeal, we ensure that every project is designed to last. Our expert team combines modern design concepts with precise engineering to create spaces that are both practical and visually striking.

At Al Madani Development, we are dedicated to shaping a sustainable and modern infrastructure that serves communities and contributes to Oman’s development.
